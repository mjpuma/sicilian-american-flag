Bring the Sicilian-American flag home. Premium fabric, crisp print, and the fully detailed device (gold cross, traditional Trinacria, Archimedes’ spiral) in the USA-blue canton. The field uses Palermo-red and white stripes for high legibility outdoors and on camera. Arranged in a loose triangle, the three devices quietly echo the island’s three-caped form—a modern triskelion of symbols.

Production Note

The flagship design uses white stripes for clarity and the device in gold for Sicilian warmth. For lapel pins, small details are simplified to keep lines clean at ~22 mm. If you release a yellow-stripe flag variant later, invert the device to white for contrast.
