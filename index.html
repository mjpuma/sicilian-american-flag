Quick Blurb

“Uniting Sicilian ingenuity with American opportunity, this flag celebrates heritage, creativity, and the freedom to thrive.”

Formal Introduction

The Sicilian‑American flag blends the island’s enduring heritage with the freedoms secured across the Atlantic. A deep American‑blue canton frames two white emblems: an Archimedean spiral—honoring centuries of Sicilian creativity—and a wheat‑free Trinacria affirming self‑determination. Nine alternating red and yellow stripes, inspired by the historic colors of Palermo and Corleone, pay tribute to each province while symbolizing courage and prosperity. Together these elements create a banner that is proudly Sicilian, proudly American, and boldly forward‑looking.
