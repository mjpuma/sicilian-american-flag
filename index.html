“Uniting Sicilian ingenuity with American opportunity, this flag celebrates heritage, creativity, and the freedom to thrive.”

The Sicilian‑American flag blends the island’s enduring heritage with the freedoms secured across the Atlantic. A deep American‑blue canton frames a radiant sunburst with cross—evoking the divine light and Christian tradition found in Sicilian churches. Nine blue, white, and red stripes represent the nine provinces of Sicily. The red stripe beneath the canton honors Palermo, Sicily’s historic capital and revolutionary heart.

This design honors centuries of maritime tradition, from Sicily’s fishing culture to the immigrant journey across the Atlantic. The sunburst draws inspiration from Baroque altarpieces in Palermo, Ragusa, and Noto, where radiant light symbolized divine presence.
