Introduction

This version of the Sicilian-American flag integrates bold Palermo red stripes and a golden cross-Trinacria set against a blue canton that mirrors the United States flag. The design evokes strength, sacred tradition, and the journey across the sea toward freedom.

The golden Trinacria cross—adapted to emphasize Sicily’s Christian heritage—radiates against USA blue, symbolizing faith carried across the ocean. The red and white stripes represent the nine historic Sicilian provinces and also echo the visual rhythm of the American flag, grounding this design in both origin and destination.
