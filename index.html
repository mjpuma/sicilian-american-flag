“Uniting Sicilian tradition with American liberty, this flag celebrates cultural roots, Christian heritage, and the journey across oceans in pursuit of freedom.”

Symbolism and Story

ElementMeaning
Blue cantonAmerican liberty and Sicily’s maritime past—representing the transatlantic journey and our oceanic identity
White sunburst with crossDraws from Baroque altarpieces in Palermo, Ragusa, and Noto—symbolizing divine light, the Holy Trinity, and Sicily’s Christian legacy dating back to apostolic times
Nine horizontal stripesEach stripe represents one of the nine provinces of Sicily, with red highlighting Palermo as the cultural and revolutionary heart
Red stripe beneath cantonConnection to Palermo, spiritual center of Sicilian resistance and artistic renewal

Palermo’s Chiesa del Gesù—sunburst above the tabernacle

Ragusa’s San Giuseppe—radiant domes and divine light

Noto’s Cathedral—Baroque illumination and divine grandeur
