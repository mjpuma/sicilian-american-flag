Introduction

The Sicilian‑American flag honors the ancient heritage of Sicily and the spiritual and cultural journey of its people to the United States. At its heart is a radiant nine-rayed sunburst with a Christian cross — evoking Sicily’s deep Christian roots, its baroque artistic traditions, and the Holy Trinity. The nine rays represent the nine provinces of Sicily, casting their light across time and ocean toward a new life in America.

Alternating stripes of blue, white, and red signify the maritime legacy of Sicily, the transatlantic journey, and the enduring values of courage and liberty. The deep blue canton represents the American home — a land of opportunity, refuge, and freedom. This flag embodies faith and resilience across generations.
